Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?
Go to the Profile screen in the app and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your personal data, recipes, and household information.
How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store. On iPhone, go to Settings > your name > Subscriptions. On Android, open the Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions. From there you can change or cancel your plan.
I'm not receiving the sign-in code
Check your spam or junk folder. If the email still doesn't arrive, make sure you're entering the correct email address and try again after a few minutes. If the problem persists, contact us at support@kitchup.app.
Can I share recipes with my household?
Yes. Once you're part of a household, all members can view and edit shared recipes, meal plans, and shopping lists.
How do I request a copy of my data?
Send an email to privacy@kitchup.app and we'll provide an export of your personal data in accordance with GDPR.
I found a bug or something isn't working
Please email support@kitchup.app with a description of what happened, what you expected, and your device model and OS version. Screenshots are very helpful.
